Our advantages

Unique conditions

Often popular decentralized projects reserve about 60% of tokens in order to have more influence during decision-making. We distribute 87% of assets among our users.

Decision making

In order to put forward a proposal, 0.5% of the delegated tokens are needed, and 4% is needed to reach a quorum on any proposal.

Wide selection

Various liquidity pools are available to you on two blockchains: Ethereum and Tron.

Safety

All funds are stored on your personal wallets without transferring private keys to third parties. Thus, they are always safe.
