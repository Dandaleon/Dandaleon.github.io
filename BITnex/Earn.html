Deposit

This product used to be called SMART-staking. You save your cryptocurrency investments on Airtex, and the exchange uses the assets in margin trading. For this you get interest.

That is, a crypto deposit is an alternative to a bank deposit, but in cryptocurrency and with broader contribution possibilities.

Staking

With your investments you participate in performing various network functions in exchange for remuneration (fixed or in the form of interest). Your cryptocurrency becomes part of the Proof-of-Stake process, that is, it provides verification and protection of all transactions without the participation of a bank or payment processor and receives income for it.
